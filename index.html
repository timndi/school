ADMISSION TO PRIMARY SCHOOL

As an international school, we are proud to provide the highest possible standard of education to our pupils, enabling them to compete in the increasingly competitive global environment.

The need for us to maintain this standard demands is not just that teaching methods are appropriate to the needs of our students but that those students, in turn, are able to demonstrate that they have the potential or ability to achieve the levels we require.

Admission is not based on ethnic, religious or social grounds.

ADMISSION TO NURSERY

Admission is on, all year round as long as the child had reached the age of 18 months.

Admission Procedure:

STEP 1. Obtain an application form, fill and Submit together with the photocopy of original birth certificate and passport photographs.

STEP 2. A brief interview will be conducted by the Head of school with the candidate and parents.

STEP 3. The offer of provisional admission is made to successful candidates.
